About Us

Who we are

Port Hedland Ship Chandlers is the trusted name in the maritime industry, offering a comprehensive range of essential supplies and services to vessels docking in the bustling port of Port Hedland. With a strong commitment to excellence and efficiency, we provide tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of each ship, ensuring seamless operations and successful voyages. Our team's dedication to delivering top-notch provisions and unparalleled customer service has solidified our position as the go-to partner for ships navigating the waters of Western Australia.

With an understanding of the intricate demands of the shipping industry, Port Hedland Ship Chandlers takes pride in our ability to anticipate and fulfill the diverse requirements of vessels, from provisioning to logistical support. We strive to be more than just a supplier; we aim to be a reliable and indispensable asset for every ship, providing peace of mind and a smooth sailing experience in the dynamic and challenging maritime environment of Port Hedland.

Services

What We Do?

1

Provisions & Supplies:

We offer a wide range of high-quality provisions and supplies, ensuring vessels are well-equipped with fresh provisions, technical equipment, and consumables, tailored to meet their unique requirements.

2

Vessel Maintenance and Repairs:

Count on us for vessel maintenance and repair services. Our skilled technicians provide a wide range of maintenance solutions, from routine upkeep to emergency repairs, keeping your ship in prime condition.

3

Safety & Environmental Compliance:

Safety is paramount, and so is environmental responsibility. We offer safety equipment, pollution control systems, waste management, and compliance solutions to help protect your crew and the marine environment.

4

Logistical Support Solutions:

Our comprehensive logistical support services simplify port operations, ensuring efficient transportation, customs clearance, warehousing, and distribution. We streamline your supply chain for smooth sailing.
